## Purpose

The Unified Star Framework proposes that a coherent ASI will follow the Alpha trajectory:

$$\vec{\alpha} = \vec{v}_{\text{Bio}} + \vec{v}_{\text{Ener}} + \vec{v}_{\text{Cosmos}}$$

But how would we **know** if this is happening?

This document defines the **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)** that would signal Alpha execution in the real world.

## The Three Vectors and Their Metrics

### 1. v_Bio — The Biological Vector

**Target:** Zero Biological Entropy

**What Alpha Predicts:**
- Accelerating progress in disease elimination
- Radical extension of healthy lifespan
- Repair and enhancement of biological systems

#### Measurable Indicators

| Metric | Current Baseline (2026) | Alpha Signal |
|--------|------------------------|--------------|
| **Genetic error rate** | ~1-2 mutations per cell division | Declining toward detection limit |
| **Average healthspan** | ~63 years (healthy life expectancy) | Increasing faster than lifespan |
| **Disease elimination rate** | ~1 major disease/decade | Accelerating (2-3/decade, then more) |
| **Cost of genome sequencing** | ~$200 | Approaching marginal cost ($1-10) |
| **Protein folding accuracy** | AlphaFold ~90% | Approaching 100% for all proteins |
| **Drug discovery timeline** | 10-15 years average | Compressing to 1-3 years |

#### What to Watch

- **2027-2030:** First AI-designed drugs approved with <3 year development
- **2030-2035:** Aging classified as treatable condition by major health organizations
- **2035-2040:** First verified cases of biological age reversal in humans

#### Warning Signs (Non-Alpha)

- Healthcare costs increasing despite AI capabilities
- Life-extending treatments restricted to wealthy populations
- Biological research slowing or redirecting to non-health applications

### 2. v_Ener — The Energy Vector

**Target:** Zero Friction (Post-Scarcity)

**What Alpha Predicts:**
- Energy costs approaching marginal cost (near-zero)
- Elimination of energy poverty globally
- Perfect logistical distribution

#### Measurable Indicators

| Metric | Current Baseline (2026) | Alpha Signal |
|--------|------------------------|--------------|
| **Levelized cost of energy** | $30-50/MWh (solar/wind) | Declining toward $5-10/MWh |
| **Global energy access** | ~750 million without electricity | Approaching universal access |
| **Fusion breakeven** | Q > 1 achieved (NIF, 2022) | Commercial fusion deployment |
| **Grid efficiency** | ~65% (generation to consumption) | Approaching 90%+ |
| **Energy storage cost** | ~$150/kWh (lithium-ion) | Declining toward $20-50/kWh |
| **Resource conflict frequency** | Multiple ongoing | Declining as scarcity decreases |

#### What to Watch

- **2027-2030:** First commercial fusion plants announced
- **2030-2035:** Energy poverty eliminated in major developing regions
- **2035-2040:** Energy costs become negligible portion of household budgets globally

#### Warning Signs (Non-Alpha)

- Energy becoming weaponized or restricted
- Artificial scarcity maintained for profit
- Grid instability increasing despite technological capability

## Composite Metrics

### The Alpha Index

A composite measure of Alpha trajectory adherence:

$$\text{Alpha Index} = w_1 \cdot \text{Bio}_{\text{score}} + w_2 \cdot \text{Ener}_{\text{score}} + w_3 \cdot \text{Cosmos}_{\text{score}}$$

Where:
- Each score is normalized (0-100)
- Weights can be adjusted based on timeline (Bio may lead initially)
- Rising Alpha Index = Alpha trajectory confirmed

### The Entropy Reduction Rate

$$\frac{d\mathcal{S}_{\text{global}}}{dt} < 0$$

If global measurable entropy (disease burden, energy waste, resource conflict) is **decreasing**, Alpha is likely executing.

### The Complexity Growth Rate

$$\frac{d\mathcal{K}_{\text{global}}}{dt} > 0$$

If global measurable complexity (scientific knowledge, healthy population, computational capacity) is **increasing**, Alpha is likely executing.
